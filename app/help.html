Help!

Some advice on playing the Data Dealer demo version

Your aim

  • Collect the best data delicacies from underground sources.
  • Create companies and online ventures for data collection.
  • Make great deals with your customers to finance the expansion of your empire.
  • Gather and refine profiles until your database is worth a bundle.

Rise from small-time back room dealer to mighty data mogul!

Operation and Game Interface

  • Every element can be moved around - just click, hold and drag!
  • The game board can be moved in the same way – just click on some empty background and drag in the desired direction.
  • Click on your agents, sources and tools to get more detailed information and to access tabs for upgrading your projects, funding ad campaigns, and assembling your team.
  • Click on the attributes you collected (e.g. first name) to receive some additional information and some hints on how you could use them.

Profiles

  • First-rate personality profiles consist of a large number of single facts.
  • Every dirty detail counts - your database is an intelligent machine which can derive new information from seemingly insignificant details.


Hint: The more complete your data is, the more it’s worth.

Your Data Empire

  • At the center of your empire sits your Data Vault, with your network around it.
  • Gather profiles: The blue lines lead from your sources into your database.
  • Sell profiles: Orange lines connect out to the clients who buy your data.

Hint: You can move and arrange all elements however you like.

Underworld

  • If you regularly supply your sources with a little cash, they’ll occasionally cough up small (or large!) packs of profiles.
  • When you “Collect” profiles from sources they’ll be sent to the Database to await integration.
  • Your status is only updated once you’ve integrated the new profiles in your database.

Hint: Before adding a set of profiles to your database, decide which profile attributes you need most at that moment.

Data tools & online ventures

  • To keep your ventures active and happily gathering profiles, you need to invest in them regularly.
  • Upgrade your tool’s capabilities, advertise, and assemble a specialized team. All of these will raise your output of profiles.
  • Some upgrades earn you new attributes or other benefits.

Hint: Upgrades usually increase the cost of general operation (the cost to Invest).

Make Money

  • Help enterprises, banks, insurance companies, human resource departments and state agencies to make their decisions.
  • Offer them your database in exchange for a little cash.
  • Make great deals and watch the money roll in.
  • The more profiles you have, the more money you make.

Hint: Take a look at which attributes your customers are especially interested in.

Your database

  • Your database holds all the profile attributes you’ve gathered so far.
  • After you’ve collected new profiles from your sources or projects, you need to integrate them in your database!
  • Once that is done, you will know how many of those profiles are completely new and how many relate to information you already had.
  • Your database is more than just a powerful data storage device! It’s an intelligent machine that can perfectly tie all that data together.

Hint: The orange bar beneath profile attributes shows where you need fresh supplies.

Risk

  • Is all this data completely legal? Far from it!
  • Every data source increases your risk. Should your risk factor get too high, the general public, action groups or privacy advocates might become a pain in the neck.
  • Every data source has a risk factor between 0 and 10, which is added to your overall risk every time you collect from them.

Hint: Whenever you upgrade a project, check how this will influence your risk.

Energy & Experience

  • Each day only has 24 hours, and your energy is limited – make it count!
  • Don’t worry if your energy runs out again. Just wait a little while and soon you’ll be fit as a fiddle again.
  • Every action earns you experience and gets you closer to the next level.

Hint: If you make it to the next level, the rush of endorphins will pep you up right away.

Hints for beginners

  • Select a client and check which profile attributes they need most.
  • Collect as many profiles as possible with those precise attributes.
  • This way you can quickly cash in larger and larger sums and gradually expand your empire.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Why do I start the game with over a million profiles?

In our demo, you start in the middle of the game and already have some profiles, attributes, projects and underworld sources.

Can I save my game progress?

We’re sorry, but this demo version sadly forgets all your accomplishments as soon as you close your browser window. In the full game, you’ll be able to log in (with or without a Facebook account) and tend to your data empire over a longer period of time even if you decide to take a break now and then.

Why can’t I get rid of project upgrades?

We plan to implement this feature in the full game. As already mentioned, this is only a demo. Um. ;)
